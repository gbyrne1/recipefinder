Pesto Pasta


Ingredients:

  • pasta
  • basil leaves
  • parmesan cheese
  • pine nuts
  • garlic
  • olive oil

1 lb pasta, 2 cups fresh basil leaves, 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup pine nuts, 2 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup olive oil. 1. Cook pasta according to package directions. 2. In a food processor, combine basil, parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic. 3. Pulse until ingredients are finely chopped. 4. Gradually add olive oil and continue pulsing until a paste is formed. 5. Drain pasta and toss with pesto sauce. 6. Serve immediately.
