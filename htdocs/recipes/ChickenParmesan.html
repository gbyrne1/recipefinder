Chicken Parmesan


Ingredients:

  • chicken
  • flour
  • egg
  • panko bread crumbs
  • parmesan cheese
  • olive oil
  • marinara sauce
  • mozzarella cheese

1 lb spaghetti 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips 1 cup all-purpose flour 2 eggs, beaten 1 cup panko bread crumbs 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese 1/2 cup olive oil 1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese 1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. 2. Dip chicken strips in flour, then egg, then bread crumbs. 3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 4. Add chicken strips and cook until golden brown. 5. Remove chicken from skillet and drain on paper towels. 6. Add spaghetti to skillet and toss with chicken. 7. Sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan cheese. 8. Serve immediately.
