Beef Stroganoff


Ingredients:

  • egg noodles
  • beef sirloin
  • onion
  • garlic
  • beef broth
  • sour cream
  • flour
  • olive oil

1 lb egg noodles, 1 lb beef sirloin, cut into thin strips, 1 onion, chopped, 2 cloves garlic, minced, 1 cup beef broth, 1 cup sour cream, 2 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp olive oil. 1. Cook egg noodles according to package directions. 2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. 3. Add beef strips and cook until browned. 4. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened. 5. Sprinkle flour over the mixture and stir to combine. 6. Add beef broth and bring to a boil. 7. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce thickens. 8. Remove from heat and stir in sour cream. 9. Drain noodles and serve with beef stroganoff sauce.
