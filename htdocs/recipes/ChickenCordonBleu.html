Recipe Name: Chicken Cordon Bleu

Rating:

Instructions:

1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 2 eggs, beaten 1 cup panko bread crumbs 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese 1/2 cup olive oil 4 slices ham 4 slices swiss cheese 1. Dip chicken strips in flour, then egg, then bread crumbs. 2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 3. Add chicken strips and cook until golden brown. 4. Remove chicken from skillet and drain on paper towels. 5. Place a slice of ham and a slice of swiss cheese on each chicken strip. 6. Roll up chicken and secure with toothpicks. 7. Return chicken to skillet and cook until cheese is melted. 8. Serve immediately.
