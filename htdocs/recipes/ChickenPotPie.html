Recipe Name: Chicken Pot Pie

Instructions:

1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 2 eggs, beaten 1 cup panko bread crumbs 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese 1/2 cup olive oil 1/2 cup butter 1/2 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 cup chicken broth 1/2 cup milk 1/2 cup frozen peas 1/2 cup frozen corn 1/2 cup carrots, diced 1/2 cup celery, diced 1/2 cup onion, diced 1/2 cup frozen green beans 1/2 cup frozen peas 1/2 cup frozen corn 1/2 cup carrots, diced 1/2 cup celery, diced 1/2 cup onion, diced 1/2 cup frozen green beans 1. Dip chicken strips in flour, then egg, then bread crumbs. 2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 3. Add chicken strips and cook until golden brown. 4. Remove chicken from skillet and drain on paper towels. 5. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. 6. Add flour and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture is golden brown. 7. Gradually add chicken broth and milk, stirring constantly. 8. Add peas, corn, carrots, celery, onion, and green beans. 9. Bring to a boil and cook until thickened. 10. Add chicken and stir to combine. 11. Serve immediately.
