Chicken Alfredo


Ingredients:

  • fettuccine
  • chicken
  • garlic
  • heavy cream
  • parmesan cheese

1 lb fettuccine ,1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 cups heavy cream 1 cup grated parmesan cheese 1. Cook fettuccine according to package directions. 2. Cook chicken in a large skillet until no longer pink. 3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. 4. Add heavy cream and parmesan cheese and cook until thickened. 5. Drain fettuccine and add to skillet with sauce. 6. Serve immediately.
